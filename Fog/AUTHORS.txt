Other libraries and source code used in Fog (always BSD/MIT/Public Domain)
==========================================================================

Chromium <http://code.google.com/p/chromium/> - Threading and Event loop related.
Ultimate++ <http://www.ultimatepp.com> - Small unicode support, inspiration.
FastDelegate <http://www.codeproject.com/cpp/FastDelegate.asp> - Delegates.

XOrg <http://www.x.org> - Region.
AggOO <http://www.dratek.com/> - ColorMatrix.
Evas <http://www.enlightenment.org> - Dithering to 8bpp, dithering tables.
AGG version 2.4 <http://www.antigrain.com> - Affine matrix, Path, Rasterizer.
GifLib <http://sourceforge.net/projects/libungif> - ImageIO GIF support.
