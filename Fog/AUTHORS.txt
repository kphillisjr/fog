Other Libraries and Sources Used in Fog
=======================================

Ultimate++ <http://www.ultimatepp.com> - Small unicode support, inspiration
FastDelegate <http://www.codeproject.com/cpp/FastDelegate.asp> - Delegates
Chromium <http://code.google.com/p/chromium/> - Threading, Event Loop, Event Pump, ...

XOrg <http://www.x.org> - Region
Evas <http://www.enlightenment.org> - Dithering
AGG version 2.4 <http://www.antigrain.com> - Vector painting, antialiased filling of primitives
GifLib <http://sourceforge.net/projects/libungif> - ImageIO GIF support
